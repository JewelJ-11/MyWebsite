About Me

Hi! Welcome to my website about Kerala! I was born on July 6, 2011, and I am the youngest child in my family, with two older brothers. I created this website because I want to share the incredible beauty of India with others, since many people don’t know much about it. In particular, I want to highlight the amazing state of Kerala, where I come from. Kerala is very special to me because my grandparents live there, and I love them very much. Visiting them is always meaningful, and I cherish the time I spend with them. I also love the beautiful nature in Kerala, from its peaceful backwaters and greenery to its calm and relaxing atmosphere. Another thing I really enjoy is the fresh and delicious food, which makes every visit even more memorable.

My Interests

I also love dancing and have been doing it for as long as I can remember. I started Bollywood dance when I was around 4 or 5 years old, and at age 7 I began learning Bharatanatyam. I continue to practice both styles today and really enjoy them. In addition, I love exploring the world and learning about different places. When I’m older, my dream is to travel across the globe and visit amazing landmarks, including the Seven Wonders of the World. I hope you enjoy learning about Kerala as much as I enjoy sharing it!
